Cross‑Ontology Alignments

Expert‑curated mappings between aidoc: terms and reference ontologies. Candidate pairs are generated by lexical similarity, classified by an LLM into SKOS mapping relations, and independently curated by three domain experts (majority vote with joint adjudication). Published here are the mappings accepted as proposed or with a curator‑corrected relation, as SKOS mapping relations with PROV‑O provenance.
